Description:

An entertaining event that tests your tech knowledge and quick thinking. So bring out the creative thinker in you by connecting the dots.

Format:

Round 1: [ Conn–ex–ions]
1. The event starts with 3-5 players.
2. You need to guess the meme from the clues given which would be in the form of movie referrals.
3. The team that identifies first will be awarded points. 4. The teams with the maximum points will enter the second round.

Round 2: [ Pictionary]
1. The top team has to send two members to draw a given meme. 2. The other teammates should guess the meme drawn.

Rules:

  1. Online registration is must.
  2. Strictly adhere to the format of the game.
