Description:

Get ready to flaunt your financial finesse at our IPL Auction event. Compete with the best and turn every penny into a powerhouse player.

Format:

Level 1:
A quiz competition in round 1 to select the top 10 teams for final round. Each correct answer will be awarded with 1 Point. Each team/player are allowed to choose only 1 option for a question.

Level 2:
IPL Auction is an event where students participate in the group of 3-4 members where they use their investment skills depending upon the budget and ongoing inflations. In IPL Auction, each team will get a virtual wallet with Rs. 100 Crores. The teams will participate in an auction and have to form up a cricket team of minimum 10 players with available funds under their Franchise. The auction will be conducted with defined rules and conditions. The teams are required to plan their resources judiciously and articulate in best interest of the team.

Rules:

  1. Each team will be allotted a budget of 100 crores.
  2. A team must buy 10 players in the Auction, otherwise they will be disqualified.
  3. The teams shall use this budget to make a team of 10 players keeping the following criteria in mind: There should be at least 3 batsmen, There should be at least 3 bowlers, There should be at least 1 All rounder, There should be at least 1 wicket-keeper.
  4. Failure in fulfilling these criteria will lead to disqualification of the team. If a team runs out of money they are disqualified.
  5. Players are rated out of 100 and each player’s ratings has been fixed and won’t be changed.
  6. There will not be reauctioning of any player in any case…teams will be disqualified if they won’t meet any of the criteria mentioned above.
  7. Till 1 cr. The bid will be increased by 10 lakh 1-5 cr, the bid will be increased by 20 lakh 5 cr and above, the bid will be increased by 50 lakhs.
  8. There are star fielders written with the names of some players...such players would be given 1 bonus point.
  9. In case of a tie among the teams, the team with the most remaining budget amount will be the winner.
